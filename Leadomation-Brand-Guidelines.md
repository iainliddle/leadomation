## 1. LOGO

### Primary Logo
- **Logo Mark:** Rounded square (10px corner radius) containing the letter "L"
- **Logo Text:** "Leadomation" positioned to the right of the mark
- **Minimum Size:** 36px height for digital, 12mm for print

### Logo Mark Specifications
- **Shape:** Square with 10px rounded corners
- **Size:** 36-38px (digital)
- **Background:** Primary gradient fill
- **Letter "L":** White, centered, bold weight (800-900)
- **Shadow:** Subtle glow `rgba(79, 70, 229, 0.25)`

### Logo Text Specifications
- **Font:** Outfit
- **Weight:** 800 (Extra Bold)
- **Size:** 22px (relative to 36px mark)
- **Color:** White (#FFFFFF) on dark backgrounds
- **Letter Spacing:** -0.5px

### Clear Space
Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "L" in all directions.

## 5. BUTTON STYLES

### Primary Button
- **Background:** Primary gradient or solid #4F46E5
- **Text:** White, 700 weight, 13-16px
- **Padding:** 16-18px vertical, 36-40px horizontal
- **Border Radius:** 12-16px
- **Shadow:** `0 4px 20px rgba(79, 70, 229, 0.3)`
- **Hover:** Slight lift (-2px), increased shadow, lighter background

### Secondary/Ghost Button
- **Background:** Transparent
- **Border:** 1px solid rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.08)
- **Text:** #F1F5F9 or #94A3B8, 600 weight
- **Padding:** 16px vertical, 36px horizontal
- **Border Radius:** 12px
- **Hover:** Border lightens, subtle background fill

### Small Button (Nav CTA)
- **Padding:** 9-10px vertical, 20-24px horizontal
- **Border Radius:** 10px
- **Font Size:** 13px

## 10. ANIMATION & MOTION

## 12. USAGE GUIDELINES

### Do
- Use the primary gradient for key brand moments
- Maintain high contrast for text readability
- Apply subtle hover animations for interactivity
- Use the emerald accent for success states and CTAs

### Don't
- Place logo on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
- Use colors outside the defined palette
- Apply heavy shadows that overpower the design
- Mix different gradient directions inconsistently
